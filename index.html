Step by step

To facilitate the first contact, it is important to gather as much information as possible.
Check out below a good step by step of making your budget.  

1

Briefing

When organizing your ideas to make a budget, it is important that you have clarity on the main information of the work. Here are some examples of important characteristics in a job:
Theme: (Sci-fi, Western, Horror, Medieval, Futuristic, Space)
Character: (Hero, Villain, Orc, Ghost, Goblin, Humanoid)
Setting: (Fantasy, Time of day, Epoch, Civilization Type)
2

References

After defining your brief it's time to gather some references, references are essential to know what kind of work you want. See important types of references for a good understanding of your idea:
Style: Style references are meant to define the direction your art will take
(SplashArt, Comics, Magic, Cartoon)
Indirect: Indirect references are linked to the feeling of your illustration, all images that help you convey the message and feeling you want in your art.
3

Sending E-mail

This part is the easiest, now just send the base of your project and leave the rest to me. :)
With a briefing rich in information and good references, it is a guarantee of success!
